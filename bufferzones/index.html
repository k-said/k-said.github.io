Buffer Zones Demo

Welcome to the Buffer Zones Demo application. Please follow these steps:

  1. Upload or edit your rules CSV file (CP products and bufferzones rules)
  2. Upload or edit parameters file (OpenStreetMap tags and headers of CSV)
  3. Select CP products to spray
  4. Upload or draw your field map
  5. Click "Submit" to generate results
  6. Download the buffer zone once results are displayed

For questions or suggestions, please contact Said Kharbouche.
